  Stacks are simple linear data structures that used to store and retrieve data easily.



Stacks are based on LIFO(Last in First Out) or FILO(First In Last Out) principal. Two main operations can be performed on stacks, and they are known as;

  1. Push (Add an element)
  2. Pop (Remove an element)
